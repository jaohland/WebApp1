Welcome

Counter-Strike: Global offensive is a FPS shooter created by Valve, a game company that is based in Seattle, Washington. There are terrorists and counter-terrorists, where the terrorists are trying to plant a bomb in a designated site, while the counter-terrorists are trying to defuse the bomb or kill all the terrorists, whichever happens first. If you're looking the ins and outs of Counter Strike that all the pros use than you came to the right place. This website is dedicated to helping players that want to start getting serious about the competitive scene.

Tip of the week

while using a rifle such as the m4a1-s, m4a4, or ak47, make sure to one tap instead of holding down the shooting button. This helps you control the grouping of your bullets, and will help you hit your target.
